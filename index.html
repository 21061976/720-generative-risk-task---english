üéØ Project Goals (3 Core Objectives)

1. Implementation of a Process-Based Personalized Teaching System
Development and implementation of a comprehensive personalized learning system including 6 core processes: learner introduction and mapping, goal setting, dynamic learning, assessment and reflection, learning process mentoring, and supportive environment
2. Development of an Advanced AI-Based Technological System
Building a comprehensive technological infrastructure including integration between physical and digital learning, advanced analytics, and dynamic personalization of content and learning methods for each student
3. Development of Agency and 21st Century Competencies Based on the Learning Compass 2030
Nurturing student agency through anticipation-action-reflection cycles, and developing transformative competencies including creating new value, addressing dilemmas, and taking responsibility

üöÄ Innovation Regulation

Project "Personalized Learning 720" represents a pedagogical-technological revolution aligned with Responsible Research and Innovation (RRI) principles and Learning Compass 2030 requirements

Innovation Level Definition

The project represents a paradigmatic shift in the educational system, transitioning from a traditional teaching model to a complex, personalized process-based system. Innovation is expressed in the development of six core processes supporting student agency and 21st century competencies, while integrating advanced technologies such as artificial intelligence and multi-dimensional analytics.

üë•Innovation Regulation Professional Team Recommendation:

Recommended to approve an expanded pilot in 20 educational institutions for two years, while conducting a controlled experiment with a control group. Approval conditions: (1) Development of an advanced competency assessment system, (2) Comprehensive training for educational teams, (3) Full technological support, (4) Accompanying academic research to examine long-term effects

üìù Executive Summary and Recommendations

Executive Summary: Personalized Learning Project Overview

Project "Personalized Learning 720" represents a comprehensive pedagogical-technological revolution based on the Learning Compass 2030. The system includes six core processes for developing agency and 21st century competencies, while integrating advanced technologies. Innovation level is high (8.8/10) with revolutionary pedagogical impact and significant technological complexity. Key risks relate to resistance to change and technological complexity, but opportunities to lead global education and improve student achievement are significant.

Key Recommendations for the Committee:

  • 1. Approve Controlled Pilot: Approve a controlled experiment in 20 schools for two years to test system effectiveness in implementing personalized learning processes (Goal 1)
  • 2. Invest in Technological Development: Allocate budget of 80 million NIS for developing the advanced technological system and required infrastructure (Goal 2)
  • 3. Comprehensive Accompanying Research: Establish an inter-academic research committee to track agency and competency development, with innovative learning metrics (Goal 3)
  • 4. Extensive Professional Training: Develop a multi-year training program for educational teams, including close mentoring and development of professional learning communities
  • 5. Regulatory Reduction Mechanisms: Create a flexible framework enabling project implementation while reducing bureaucratic burden and providing pedagogical freedom
  • 6. Monitoring and Steering Committee: Establish a professional steering committee to oversee the project, including academic representatives, technology experts, and educational leaders
